Sustainable mosquito nets with a microstructure that repels insects

Insecta is a company inspired by nature. We're looking for new solutions and incorporate biomimicry elements into the design of mosquito nets. Our product is made of incerdibly strong spider silk blended with polycaprolactone. In order to make our nets even more effective, the product has papillae obtained from pitcher plant. Thanks to them, a slippery surface is created and mosquitoes are repelled. Spray our product with a small amount of water and see how it works!
