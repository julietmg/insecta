Every net counts.

Countless lives are being saved every year due to the help of Insects' nets. Mosquito nets help protect us from these insects and the diseases they transmit. Unfortunately, widely used nets can be easily torn, and hard to repair. They get often replaced with a new one which leads to the build up of plastic in the environment.

Insecta mosquito nets are produced from spider silk blended with polycaprolactone. Papillae obtained from pitcher plant efficiently repel mosquitos and make the product even more effective. We take care of the environment and people and as a result our nets are biodegradable, sustainable and safe.

We support the charity

We want to help the world and people and that is why we support the Jungle Fever Opponents. This nonprofit organisation works globally and has affiliate organisations based in UK, Poland, Germany and the Spain. We believe that the purpose of this foundation to create a world where not a single person dies from an infected mosquito bite is of great importance and we are proud to help in acheiving such beautiful goal.

Important goals of the charity are to increase the worldwide distribution of life saving mosquito nets, anti malarial drugs and to strengthen health systems to accurately diagnose and treat malaria. Jungle Fever Opponents wants to fight dengue fever, zika virus as well as other mosquito borne diseases. Endemic countries that they are currently running programs in: Cameroon, Kenya, Nigeria and India.
